Jaika Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Jaika is a self-hosted AI platform. It is free, open-source, and runs on personal hardware. There is no company behind Jaika — it is an independent project.

2. What Data We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • Your Google user ID
  • Your email address and display name
  • An OAuth access token (used to verify your identity)

During your use of Jaika, we store:

  • Chat sessions and message history
  • Files you upload
  • Memory facts you explicitly add
  • Skills/personas you create

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your sessions and files
  • To send your prompts to the Google Gemini API to generate responses
  • To send text to the ElevenLabs API for text-to-speech (TTS) audio
  • To personalise your experience using stored memory facts and skills

Your data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than operating Jaika for you.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — your prompts are sent to Google for processing. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs — text sent for TTS is processed by ElevenLabs. See ElevenLabs Privacy Policy.
  • Google OAuth — sign-in is handled by Google. We only receive the data described in Section 2.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored on the device running Jaika. Sessions, files, memory, and skills persist until you delete them or an admin removes your account. There is no automatic expiry.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete your sessions, files, memory, and skills at any time from within the app
  • You can ask an admin to delete your account and all associated data
  • You can revoke Google OAuth access from your Google Account settings

7. Security

Jaika uses Google OAuth for authentication. All API access requires a valid user ID. Data is stored locally on the hosting device. We do not transmit your data beyond the third-party services listed above.

8. Contact

Jaika is a personal project. If you have questions or concerns, reach out to the instance administrator directly.
